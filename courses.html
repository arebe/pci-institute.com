Courses

Online Courses

Understanding Federal Loopholes

The federal government assists students by providing loans with public money. This course will teach you how to structure your finances to capture the largest amount of public funds.

Technical "Compliance"

All states require schools to reveal graduation rates and job data. But don’t worry-- in this class we’ll cover the nuances of “clearly and conspicuously,” and exactly where to hide a non-searchable PDF of this information on your websites.

Policy Protection: Working with Lobbyists to Safeguard Your New Industry

Brand New! This course will teach you how to work with local politicians and lobbyist groups, including the ASPCU (Association of Private Sector Colleges and Universities) to ensure that federal and state regulations are working in your favor. This course is especially useful for students who want to develop a long-term investment strategy in For-Profit Education.

Copywriting & Obfuscation

Our writing center empowers students to craft beautifully vague copy for all of their marketing needs. Learn to explain your for-profit educational institution without revealing core elements, such as non-standard accreditation, poor job placement records, or convoluted financing plans.

Student Recruitment Strategies

Don’t Take No for an Answer! This course will give you the tools to recruit as many students as you can using time-honored high pressures sales tactics. For instance, we’ll teach you how to target older adult students through traditional advertising in newspapers and television.

Juking the Stats 101: Career Placement Numbers

Job placement is hard. Factories shut down and corporations move overseas. Your institutions should still seek to entice prospective students with the promise of opportunity. This course will teach you how to establish direct postgraduate student employment with your own organization (or your friends’) to enhance your statistical success rates.
